Join Us

Note: Please reach out to inquire about research opportunities before submitting a formal application.

We are always excited to connect with curious and motivated scientists at the undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral levels. Our research spans many disciplines, including protein biochemistry, chemical biology, microbiology, molecular biology, and bioinformatics. Scientists from diverse scientific and personal backgrounds are especially encouraged to apply.

Before applying, we encourage you to read about our Culture.

Northeastern Undergraduates and Master's Students

Occasionally, we have positions available for undergraduate and master's students, including computational biology projects. To apply, please send a resume including relevant coursework, Co-op experience, and/or previous research experience, along with a brief statement of research interests.

Prospective Graduate Students

Please apply through the CCB or Biology graduate programs. Upon admission, you will have the opportunity to rotate in the group and join if it is a good fit for your graduate training.

Prospective Postdoctoral Researchers

Please email Thiago your CV and a cover letter describing your professional background and experience, your research interests and goals for postdoctoral training, and the contact information of three academic references.

All group members are highly encouraged to apply for external funding. See our Resources page for opportunities.
