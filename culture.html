Our Values

Scientific Rigor

We are committed to careful, reproducible, and high-quality science. We take the time to do experiments right, interpret data honestly, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of scientific integrity.

Collaboration

Great science rarely happens in isolation. We actively collaborate within the lab and with colleagues across disciplines, institutions, and career stages. We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better science.

Open Science

We are committed to transparency in our research. We share data, code, and preprints openly whenever possible, and we encourage the broader scientific community to build on our work.

Diversity & Inclusion

We are committed to building a lab where scientists from all backgrounds feel welcome, respected, and supported. We believe that diversity strengthens science, and we actively work to create an inclusive environment.

Mentorship

We are invested in the growth and success of every lab member. We provide individualized mentorship, support career development at all levels, and celebrate the achievements of our team.

Work-Life Balance

We believe that sustainable science requires taking care of yourself. We respect personal time, encourage healthy boundaries, and support the mental and physical wellbeing of all lab members.

Curiosity

We encourage creative thinking, bold ideas, and the freedom to explore. Some of the best science comes from asking unexpected questions.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of honesty in our science, in our interactions with each other, and in our engagement with the broader community.
